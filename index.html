About

I am a Ph.D. student of physics at the Institute for Advanced Studies in Basic Sciences(IASBS). I have been doing research in speckle tweezers for controlling of low and high refractive index micro-particles, measurement of depletion forces in colloids, and the various applications of optical trapping, speckle analysis and 3D microscopies.
